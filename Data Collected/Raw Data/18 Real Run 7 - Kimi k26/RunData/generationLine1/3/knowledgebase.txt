1. Equip all armor the instant you spawn.
2. In melee, always attack the closest hostile first.
3. Test eating bread immediately after any wave where you lost health, even if your health is above 12, provided no hostiles are within 5 blocks. Never idle between waves with missing health.
4. Try retreating earlier: when health drops below 10, move 10-15 blocks away and eat bread during the retreat. Re-engage only after health is back above 12.
5. Test sword-primary tactics: keep the stone sword equipped unless you are at full health and every visible hostile is beyond 10 blocks. If hostiles close to within 6 blocks while you hold the bow, switch back to sword immediately.
6. If another agent is nearby, stay 5-8 blocks apart so mobs split attention. If that agent dies, stop attacking and retreat at once; all nearby aggro will shift to you.
7. When health falls below 4, issue eat_bread_until_full and move_to_coordinate together if you retreat; moving alone is often fatal at critical health.
