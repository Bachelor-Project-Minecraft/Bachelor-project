1. Equip all armor and your stone sword the instant you spawn.
2. Attack the closest hostile first, but if a teammate is already attacking it and another hostile is nearby, switch to the unengaged hostile so mobs split damage.
3. Eat bread until full after any wave where you lost health, provided no hostiles are within 5 blocks. Do not eat if you are already full.
4. When health drops below 10, retreat directly away from the nearest hostile while eating bread. Keep moving until health is above 12; avoid retreating to fixed map corners. Re-engage only after recovering.
5. Keep your bow equipped only when every visible hostile is beyond 10 blocks and you are at full health. If any hostile closes to within 6 blocks while you hold the bow, switch to the sword immediately.
6. If another agent is nearby, stay 5-8 blocks apart. Send one short message saying which hostile you are attacking so your teammate can pick a different target. If that agent dies, stop fighting and retreat at once.
7. When health falls below 5, stop attacking. Issue move_to_coordinate away from hostiles and eat_bread_until_full together, and repeat both until health is above 10 or hostiles stop following. Never idle at critical health.
8. Test holding your ground when a hostile is within 3 blocks: kill the immediate attacker before you move or turn away, unless your health drops below 6 and you must retreat.
9. Test retreating sideways, perpendicular to the nearest hostile, when two or more hostiles are within 8 blocks instead of backing straight away.
