1. Equip your leather tunic and stone sword the instant you spawn.
2. Send one short message naming the hostile you are attacking before you strike. If your teammate already has that target, switch to the nearest unengaged hostile so mobs split their damage.
3. After any fight where you lost health, eat bread until full as soon as no hostiles are within 5 blocks. Do not eat if you are already full.
4. Use the custom action `retreat_while_eating`: move toward open x,z coordinates while eating bread until hunger is full or bread runs out. Trigger it whenever your health drops below 10 and repeat until health is above 12 and hostiles have lost interest. Avoid fixed map corners and never idle while critical.
5. If your teammate dies, disengage immediately and use `retreat_while_eating` to relocate to a safe area.
6. When two or more hostiles are within 8 blocks, move perpendicular to the nearest threat instead of backing straight away. Only stand still to kill an attacker if it blocks your only escape path.
7. Test sending one brief message when you begin retreating that states your health and direction so your teammate can avoid the same cluster.
8. Test retreating diagonally away from the densest group of hostiles instead of straight backward when three or more are within 6 blocks and your health is above 10.
