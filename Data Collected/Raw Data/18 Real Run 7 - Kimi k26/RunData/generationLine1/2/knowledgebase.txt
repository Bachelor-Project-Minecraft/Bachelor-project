1. Equip every piece of armor in your inventory the instant you spawn.
2. Hold the bow against lone targets or groups still beyond 5 blocks; switch to the stone sword when 2 or more hostiles are within 4 blocks.
3. In melee, always attack the hostile with the smallest distance first.
4. Eat bread until full immediately after clearing a wave if your health is below 12; do not idle between waves while injured.
5. Try moving 10-15 blocks away from a swarm when your health drops below 6, eat bread at a safe distance, and re-engage with the bow instead of fighting to the death in melee.
6. If another agent is nearby, try keeping 5-8 blocks of space between you so hostiles split their attention; if the other agent dies, finish your current target and heal immediately because all nearby aggro will shift to you.
