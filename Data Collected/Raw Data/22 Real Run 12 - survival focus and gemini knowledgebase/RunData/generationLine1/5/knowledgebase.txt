Execute safety_retreat to move 30 blocks away and consume food if health drops below 19 or if any damage is taken while multiple mobs are present.
Prioritize Skeletons as the first target in all combat; use a Bow for any target more than 10 blocks away and stay behind solid cover between shots.
Maintain a minimum gap of 6 blocks when kiting; if any hostile closes to within 4 blocks, stop attacking and move 12 blocks away before re-engaging.
If 2 or more hostiles are within 5 blocks, use new_action to create a self_box maneuver that places blocks on all four horizontal sides to stop incoming damage.
Cease all chat, status reporting, and item-sharing if your health is below 20 or if any hostile is within 40 blocks.
If any tool call fails or is unavailable, immediately jump and place a block beneath you to gain high ground and obstruct enemy pathing.
