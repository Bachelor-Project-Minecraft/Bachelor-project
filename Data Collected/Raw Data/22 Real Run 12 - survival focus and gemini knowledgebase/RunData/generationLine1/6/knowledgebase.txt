Equip all armor and weapons immediately; ensure your primary weapon is in your hand before a hostile encounter begins.
If any melee hostile is within 6 blocks, cancel all tasks and execute build_defensive_pillar to move 3 blocks up; stay on the pillar until all nearby targets are neutralized.
Execute safety_retreat to move 40 blocks away if health is below 20 or if more than 2 hostiles are within 8 blocks.
Prioritize Skeletons as the first target in all combat; use a Bow for any target more than 10 blocks away and stay behind solid cover between shots.
If an "eat" or "wait" tool is not found, immediately use new_action to create a custom action for that task or use use_item on food in the inventory.
If 2 or more hostiles are within 5 blocks and you cannot pillar, use new_action to create a "full_box" maneuver that places blocks on all four sides to stop incoming damage.
If any tool call fails, immediately jump and place a block beneath you to gain high ground and obstruct enemy pathing.
Cease all chat and status reporting if health is below 20 or any hostile is within 50 blocks; focus exclusively on tactical movement and defense.
