- Equip leather chestplate immediately upon pickup for protection.
- When hostiles approach, melee attack the closest one (smallest distance listed).
- After a hostile is defeated, immediately melee the next closest.
- Eat bread until full after taking damage or when health below 10.
- Never do nothing when hostiles are within 5 blocks; always engage.
- Prioritize melee over bow for zombies under 5 blocks.
- Test bow on skeletons or hostiles over 5 blocks when no close threats.
- After teammate dies, eat fully and scan for remaining mobs before advancing.
