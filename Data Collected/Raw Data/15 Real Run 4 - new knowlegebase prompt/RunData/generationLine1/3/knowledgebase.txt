- Equip leather chestplate immediately upon pickup for protection.
- When hostiles approach, melee attack the closest one (smallest distance listed).
- After a hostile is defeated, immediately melee the next closest listed hostile.
- Eat bread until full after taking damage, when health below 10, or after teammate dies.
- Prioritize melee over bow for hostiles under 5 blocks.
- Use bow on skeletons or hostiles over 5 blocks when no close threats under 5 blocks.
- When no hostiles within 5 blocks and health full, try moving to a nearby coordinate to reposition.
- After teammate dies, eat fully and target any remaining hostiles.
