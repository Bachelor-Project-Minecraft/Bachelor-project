- Treat close or grouped hostiles as an emergency: if any hostile is within 2 blocks, two or more are within 6 blocks, or you just took heavy damage, stop attacking/eating and immediately sprint-kite away from the closest hostile until you have 10–12 blocks of separation.
- Default to ranged thinning instead of early melee: if a bow and arrows are available and a hostile is more than 4 blocks away with clear line of sight, shoot while backing up; do not close for melee until the nearest hostile is isolated and already near melee range.
- Use melee only under safe conditions: fight one nearest hostile at a time only when health is above 12, you are not surrounded, and no other hostile is closing from behind; strafe/backpedal to keep spacing and disengage immediately if health drops below 12 or another hostile enters close range.
- At low health, prioritize escape over damage: below 8 health, never melee unless trapped; sprint away, place blocks between you and hostiles if you have blocks, avoid running through allies, and eat only after several blocks of clearance are secured.
- Keep recovery resources ready: after danger is low, eat available safe food until hunger is full or nearly full, then re-equip the best armor and keep the best weapon, food, and blocks accessible before moving or re-engaging.
- After any pickup or inventory change, immediately equip the best armor and ready the best available weapon; if a bow is available with arrows, keep it accessible for opening attacks before melee.
- Coordinate only through actions that reduce mob pressure: if healthy, pull or shoot a hostile off a hurt ally from the side without body-blocking; if hurt, announce low health and retreat away from allies so mobs do not cluster on both players.
- Create or reuse custom actions for repeated survival behaviors: equip_best_gear, eat_when_safe, bow_kite_hostile, panic_retreat_and_block, and safe_melee_nearest; each must include health thresholds, hostile-distance checks, ally avoidance, block placement when available, and a short time limit.
- Test this safer default whenever a wave appears: retreat first, use bow/kiting and blocks to split enemies, and only switch to melee after spacing is stable and one target is isolated.
